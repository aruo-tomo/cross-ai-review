# Proposal review packet — fictional example

## Mode
proposal

## Purpose and audience
Ask a small volunteer group to approve a one-month trial of a shared event calendar.

## Constraints
No budget commitment. The group decides at the next monthly meeting.

## Draft or working material
We propose a one-month trial of a shared calendar for public events. One volunteer will add confirmed events, and organizers will review entries before publication. At the end of the month, the group will decide whether the calendar reduced scheduling conflicts and saved coordination time.

## Review instructions
Review independently. Check the problem definition, intended outcome, feasibility, risks, ownership, and decision requested. Do not invent facts. Return the required review structure.
