# Article review packet — fictional example

## Mode
article

## Purpose and audience
Help first-time community gardeners choose a safe starting point for a small balcony herb garden.

## Constraints
Friendly, practical, under 250 words. Do not make health or pesticide claims.

## Draft or working material
Start with two herbs you already cook with. Basil and mint can be grown in containers with drainage holes. Put the containers where they receive light appropriate for the plant, and check the soil before watering. If you are unsure about a plant's needs in your climate, consult a local gardening source.

## Review instructions
Review independently. Do not invent citations or missing facts. Label material factual claims as verified, partially supported, unverified, or contradicted. Return: decision summary; must-fix issues; improvements; open questions; evidence status; and safe revision only where no human decision is needed.
