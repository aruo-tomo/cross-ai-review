# Ideation review packet — fictional example

## Mode
ideation

## Purpose and audience
Explore a simple way for a local club to help new members meet one another.

## Constraints
Ideas only; no promise to launch. Prefer a low-effort experiment.

## Draft or working material
Idea: pair new members with a volunteer for a ten-minute welcome conversation at each monthly gathering. Test it with five volunteers for one gathering, then ask participants whether it helped them meet someone and understand the club's next steps.

## Review instructions
Review independently. Check user value, assumptions, constraints, likely failure modes, alternatives, and the smallest useful experiment. Treat this as a speculative idea; only classify factual premises in the evidence table. Return the required review structure.
